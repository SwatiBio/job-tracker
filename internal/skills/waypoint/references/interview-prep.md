# Interview Prep

Question banks + sample answers + research checklist per role.

## Options

- **type**: Phone Screen | Technical | Behavioral | System Design | Final Round | Full Prep
- **difficulty**: Beginner | Intermediate | Advanced
- **questions**: 5 | 8 | 10 | 15 (scales with level: Junior 8, Mid 10, Senior 12, Lead 15)
- **sample answers**: bool

Max 5 questions per section.

## Structures

- **phone screen**: self-intro · motivation · background-check · logistics · candidate-Qs
- **technical**: general · technical · behavioral · role-specific · research-checklist · company-specific
- **system design**: requirements · estimation · data-model · api-design · scalability · tradeoffs
- **sample answers**: intro · experience-pitch · challenge-framework (STAR) · skills-to-highlight · Qs-to-ask
- **full prep**: technical + sample answers

## Steps

1. `waypoint jobs get <id>` — pull company, position, notes
2. `waypoint profile show --json` — pull skills, experience, education
3. Generate questions for the selected type; add company research checklist (mission, products, stack, culture)
4. If sample answers on → draft self-intro, STAR pitch, smart Qs to ask

**Done when**: every section has the target question count, research checklist includes company-specific items, STAR answers reference real experience from profile.
